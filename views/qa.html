Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to book an appointment?

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend booking an appointment to avoid waiting times, especially on weekends.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, debit cards, and mobile payment options.

How long does a typical haircut take?

A standard haircut takes about 30 minutes, but more complex styles may take longer.

Do you offer services for children?

Yes, we provide haircuts for children of all ages.

Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

Yes, please call us at least 2 hours before your appointment to reschedule.
